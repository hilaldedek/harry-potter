ABOUT US

Hogwarts, with its short name, is one of the prestigious schools of the magic realm. It was founded in the 11th century by the 4 most important magicians of the age in the 1000s. All four schoolhouses bear their surnames: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw and Salazar Slytherin.

Each new student at the school is placed in a building as a result of the Sorting Hat's choice. After this building is chosen, it is determined where each student will stay, in which dormitory they will sleep, and with whom they will teach. Students can increase or decrease points from their buildings. The Building Cup is awarded to the building with the most points at the end of the year.

Hogwarts begins training on September 1 each year and the term ends in June of the following year. Students have the option of staying at Hogwarts during the winter and spring breaks. Students staying in the castle are not taught and can participate in the Christmas celebrations. There are no classes during Easter week either, but due to increased homework as final exams are approaching, students devote their time to study. Students do not have holidays except holidays and weekends. In addition, students can visit the village of Hogsmeade (with their parents' permission) on holidays in their third year.
